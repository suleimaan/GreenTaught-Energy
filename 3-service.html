Mini-Grid Implementation

We offer rural, urban and industrial mini-grid renewable design, procurement and implementation. This ensures that maximum power consumers and unconnected community are better served with solutions that is applicable to their situation.

Most rural communities are not connected to the conventioanal grid depriving them of modern electricity and forcing them to continue heavy reliance on coal and wood burning. Once they have clean and easy energy alternative to coal burning and defirestation, it will be easy elminating carbon emission from our rural communities.

Some urban communities are also in dire need of alternative to grid, so we have this service in place to serve them better

Also industries as stated earlier need dedicated custom mini-grid to utilize the enormous energy demand required by their businesses. Industries are maximum consumer of electricity so this our service and product is just the ideal fit for them only that, the initial cost maybe a bit high for some industries. we take this into account by striving to facilitate adoption of clean energy by most industries.
